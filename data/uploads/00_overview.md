# Overview

Fictional organization: **Northstar Benefits Group (NBG)**

Domain: **Fintech / insurance benefits administration SaaS**

Northstar Benefits Group operates a SaaS platform called **Polaris Claims Hub**, used by mid-market employers, brokers, and third-party administrators to intake employee benefit claims, validate eligibility, route evidence to adjudication teams, and synchronize claim outcomes with payroll and carrier systems. The uploaded document set is designed to look like an internal production knowledge base for a regulated software organization preparing a major workflow modernization release called **Project Meridian**.

The files include product requirements, architecture notes, incident response materials, operations guidance, compliance rules, business review artifacts, meeting decisions, customer onboarding instructions, support knowledge base material, and roadmap planning. Facts are intentionally distributed across documents with overlapping terminology and a few realistic ambiguities, so a RAG system can be tested on grounded retrieval, comparison, citation, missing-evidence handling, and conflict detection.
